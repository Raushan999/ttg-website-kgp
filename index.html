"Technology Transfer Group brings to you an opportunity to contribute to Kharagpur's rich heritage and be a part of a group that conducts events like TEDx, bridge the gap between academia and industries, and promotes the research culture in IIT Kharagpur."

Technology Transfer Group (TTG) is a students' initiative under the aegis of Sponsored Research & Industrial Consultancy, IIT Kharagpur, which believes in the potential of the Institute as a premier Research & Development centre. We act as a link between the industry and the academia to facilitate transfer of industry ready technologies and presenting IIT Kharagpur as a research consultant.
